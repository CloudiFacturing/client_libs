How to add the WS on Visual Studio:

1. Right click on Reference
2. Choose "Add service reference..."
3. Click on advanced (bottom-left)
4. Click on "Add Web Reference..."
5. Add the URL: "https://api.eu-cloudflow.eu:443/sintef/authManager/AuthManager?wsdl"
WARNING: think to add the ":443"
6. Valid (the right arrow after the URL zone)
7. Choose a correct "web reference name" (avoid the dots & the special characters)
8. Click on OK and enjoy :)
